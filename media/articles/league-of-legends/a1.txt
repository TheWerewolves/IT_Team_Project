League of Legends’ latest game mode has proved insanely popular since it hit the live servers, with Summoners around the world getting to grips with Riot’s take on the popular Auto Chess genre.
Teamfight Tactics pits up to seven opponents against each other in an all-out war that requires both wits and strategic thinking to come out on top. The random nature of each game’s champion pool allows for some nail-bitingly tense games, especially during the mid to late-game where certain team comps start to ramp up the damage.
